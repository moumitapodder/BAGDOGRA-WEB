Bagdogra Airport (IATA: IXB, ICAO: VEBD) is a customs airport located in the western part of Siliguri, at Bagdogra in Siliguri subdivision in northern West Bengal, India. It is operated as a civil enclave at AFS Bagdogra of the Indian Air Force.

services

restaurant

The restaurant at Bagdogra, the gateway to North Bengal and Sikkim, delivers more than you expect from small-town airport eateries.The Bagdogra Airport Restaurant Workers Co-operative Society Ltd runs this restaurant (which flaunts a runway view) that can seat approximately 150 people at once. It's a place that's inviting; the food and the staff make one feel all the more welcome.

Refreshment

Free trolley and baggage services are easily available for the passengers. Physically challenged and aged people also get wheel chairs. You can carry your heavy luggage on free baggage trolleys very easily from one terminal to another. Places to Shop and Eat: There are many food counters in the airport to serve the travelers needs. Snack Bar, Tea Coffee bar and Juice Counter are some of the counters which offer you good quality food and beverages. Travelers can also get a variety of snacks.

DIGITAL SECURITY

Bank ATM and business centre with WIFI are also available in this Airport.
